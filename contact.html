Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a news tip or story idea?

You can submit news tips or story ideas by emailing our editorial team at tips@stackly.com. Please include as much detail as possible, including any supporting documents or sources. Our team reviews all submissions daily.

How do I subscribe to your newspaper?

You can subscribe to STACKLY through our website by visiting the subscription page. We offer digital-only, print-only, and combined digital+print subscription options with various billing cycles to suit your needs.

How can I advertise with STACKLY?

For advertising inquiries, please contact our advertising department at ads@stackly.com or call +91 98765 43212. We offer various advertising options including print, digital, sponsored content, and newsletter placements.

How do I report an error in an article?

If you've spotted an error in one of our articles, please email corrections@stackly.com with the article title, publication date, and details about the correction needed. We appreciate your help in maintaining our journalistic standards.

Do you offer internships or training programs?

Yes, we offer internship programs for journalism students and recent graduates. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. Please send your resume and writing samples to careers@stackly.com with "Internship Application" in the subject line.
